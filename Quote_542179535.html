XeniApp : Quote

Created by User d8ec2, last modified on Jun 09, 2021

Suggested API flow:

  1. Authenticate to the API

  2. Quick Quoting

  3. Full Quote or Eligibility

  4. Purchase

2.a. Quoting

This will be the main call you will make to get pricing & coverage information. This can also validate and check eligibility of the policy.

There are 2 forms of quoting:

  • Quick: Get product & pricing information (premium, fees, coverage) with only as much information that is needed to quote. This is indicated by setting the isQuickQuote property to true.

  • Full: Gets a price and checks eligibility in addition to product information based on coverage information. All information needed to purchase must be supplied or validation will fail. This is indicated by setting the isQuickQuote property to false.

Product Id

Use the product id to identify which product you want to quote. In the case of Vacation Rental products, use packageName as productId is not yet supported for these.

Quote Sample Request

Location

POST /v1/quote

Headers

Content-Type: application/json
Authorization: Bearer {your-token.from-the-authentication.response-here}
