Milestone 1

Upon THF receiving a complete Grant Disbursement Invoice, and the following milestones having been met to Grantee’s satisfaction in its sole discretion:

  1. Configure XeniPay SDK on Hedera Testnet and test use case; and

  2. Create (and test with) 500 wallets on the Hedera Network and verify test transactions on HCS (settlement process).

 

 

Milestone 2

Upon THF receiving a complete Grant Disbursement Invoice, and the following milestones having been met to Grantee’s satisfaction in its sole discretion:

  1. Create (and test with) 25,000 wallets on the Hedera Network; and

  2. Create Xeni rewards program for Hedera wallet users.

 

 

Milestone 3

Upon THF receiving a complete Grant Disbursement Invoice, and the following milestones having been met to Grantee’s satisfaction in its sole discretion:
