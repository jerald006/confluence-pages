Introduction

Integrate with RCI provider to offer their content in a specific section of XENI APP

Purpose

Include into the services to XENIÂ´s users the option of long stay in RCI Units as Hot Deals.

Scope

  • Add a new section in XENI app to include Hot deal based on the RCI content

    • Resort properties by Country/Region.

    • Long stays opportunities on certain period of times

  • Include super-admin logic:

    • Separate Tab for hot deals reports.

    • Hold reservation management. (Setup the time before expiring 10 or 18 ) set the xeni conditions

    • Commission logic for Hot deals. (Markup).

  • Generate the connection with the provider through the XML API.

    • Production: We need to declare the IPs address that will connect with the API.

  • Create the proper design to hold the RCI content.

  • Generate logic for B2B consumers and B2C consumers

  • Create a new section in Command center to admin the Hot Deals section.

  • Generate reports for Super-admin users related to Hot deals activities.
