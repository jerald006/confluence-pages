***Under Construction***

Why Tracking Data?

In the Digital world that provide services to customer to develop their business, it is important to analyze the behavior and the activity in the portal for the following:

  • Identify customer behaviors and interests.

  • Identify business opportunities

  • Identify what is wrong and what could be improved, weaknesses, and strengths.

  • Get ideas for marketing campaigns to focus on a product or set of products.

  • Performance of the product.

  • Growth capacity.

  • Scalability.

Metrics that matters

  • Top Destination consumptions

    • Region, market, City pairs

    • Highest date of consumption

    • Most type of Fares sold (Economy, Premium Economy, Business, First)

      • % of ancillaries includes

      • % of seat selected

      • % of Form of payments

    • Top Customers

      • What they sold

      • Region, market, city pairs (Related to point 1 of above)

    • % of sales per vendor / Market

    • Booking avg per customer (Searches vs booking created)

    • Ticketing avg per customer (Booking Ticketed vs Booking canceled)
