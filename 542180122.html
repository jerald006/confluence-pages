XeniApp : Sprint 2 Review & Retrospective

Created by Kavya Tirumalaraju, last modified on Dec 01, 2021

Review:

Accomplishments:

  1. Upgrades of MongoDB driver to 4.1.X. and Mongoose to 6.0.12 which are latest versions

  2. Upgrade of Terraform from 1.0.10 to 3.11.0

  3. Booking Tab Details for flights, hotels, and cars

 Challenges:

  1. A lot of tickets in code-review stage were left unattended. The team proposed to have another swim lane like ‘Dev Deployed’

  2. A lot of issues spilled over from Sprint 1 hence the team had 92 issues to work on in this sprint which was a bit overwhelming

 Metrics: Overall the team completed 60 out of 92 issues in this sprint. Details listed below:

  1. Team completed 13 out of 20 stories and have 3 to be merged to Prod

  2. Team worked on 13 tasks besides the above stories

  3. Team addressed 34 out of 53 bugs and have 9 bugs to be merged to Prod

  4. Team also addressed 3 tech debt issues
