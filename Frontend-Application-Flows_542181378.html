Onboarding

Agency Owner

Credentials :- Xeniap - Online Hotel, Flight, Car Booking Agency

  1. Go to the XeniApp site and click on the “Build now” option.

  2. Subscription form will open.

  3. Enter the name of account owner and mail id and password of their account.

  4. Enter the agency website name(Subdomain name)

  5. Enter the agency name.

  6. Enter the address.

  7. Select the country and enter the contact detail.

  8. Enter the customer support mail id.

  9. Click on the proceed .

  10. Enter the card payment detail and accept the terms and condition.

  11. Click on the proceed button and agency will create and also create the wallet also.

Agency Customer

Credential: - Xeniap - Online Hotel, Flight, Car Booking Agency

  1. Go to the Agency link.

  2. Click on the signup option.

  3. Enter the details.

  4. Select the checkbox for accept the terms and condition.

  5. Click on the signup button.

  6. Verification email will send on entered mail id.

  7. Go to that mail.

  8. Click on the “verify email” option.

  9. Go to the Agency link.

10. Click on the “login” option.

11. Enter the credential.

12. Click the login button and customer can login successfully.

13. Then see the customer dashboard where customer can book the Flight ,hotel ,Car.

XeniApp Customer

Credential: - Xeniap - Online Hotel, Flight, Car Booking Agency

  1. Go to the XeniApp link.

  2. Click on the “Login” button

  3. Then Click on the “signup” option.

  4. Enter the details.

  5. Select the checkbox for accept the terms and condition.

  6. Click on the signup button.

  7. Verification email will send on entered mail id.

  8. Go to that mail.

  9. Click on the “verify email” option.

  10.  Go to the XeniApp link.

  11. Click on the “login” option.

  12. Enter the credential.

  13. Click the login button and customer can login successfully.

  14. Then see the customer dashboard where customer can book the Flight ,hotel ,Car.

Sales Agent by Promotion

  1. Go to the agency dashboard.

  2. Click on the “Client” menu.

  3. Click on the “Make sales agent”.

  4. Then mail send on that client’s mail id.

  5. Verify that mail and it will redirect to the agency dashboard.

  6. And start the booking of flights ,hotel ,car.

Credential: - Xeniap - Online Hotel, Flight, Car Booking Agency

 Sales Agent by Invitation

  1. Go to the agency dashboard.

  2. Click on the “Agent” menu.

  3. Click on the “Invite Agent” option.

  4. Enter the email address.

  5. Click on the “send invite”.

  6. Go to the mail id which entered for request.

  7. Open that mail.

  8. Click on the “Sign up now” option.

  9. Enter the details and click on “sign up” button.

10.    Enter the all personal details and click on the “save “option.

11.    Go to the dashboard and start the booking of flights ,hotel ,car.

Credential: - Xeniap - Online Hotel, Flight, Car Booking Agency

Flight Booking 

By Agency Owner

  1. Go to the “Book now” option.

  2. Search the flight which customer wants to go.

  3. After found the searching detail select the one of the suitable flight.

  4. Click on the view button.

  5. Shows the information of flight name and date, destination of that booking.

  6. Enter the passenger’s detail.

  7. Enter the card payment detail.

  8. Enter the billing detail.

  9. Click on the confirm booking.

  10. Ticket information show.

  11. Send ticket detail email to customer.

  12. Go to the “Booking” option.

  13. Click on the “ticket” option on the entry which you create booking, it will change the status in the “ticket Requested”.

Note: - in email Itinerary is not need to have the register mail id. They can send the email to anyone. 

Hotel Booking

By Agency Owner

  1. Go to the “Book now” option.

  2. Select the hotel booking.

  3. Search the hotel.

  4. Select the sorting option like, cheapest to largest price of hotel will show.

  5. Select the “view detail” option which hotel u wants to see.

  6. Click on the price detail.

  7. Click on the “Book now” option.

  8. Enter the name of that person who stay in that hotel.

  9. Enter the card details.

  10. Booking will confirm and see this in “Booking” Menu. 

Car Booking

By Agency Owner

  1. Go to the “Book now” option.

  2. Select the Car booking option.

  3. Search the car by entering information in field.

  4. After got the search result use the filter for gets required trip result.

  5. Click on “View Deal” option.

  6. After showing the detail click on the “Book now” option.

  7. Enter the driver’s detail.

  8. Enter the Card details for payment.

  9. Select the terms and condition.

  10. Click on the “Pay securely” option.

  11. Details will see and mail also sends on respected mail id.

  12. Then go to the “Booking” menu.

  13. Select the “category: car”.

  14. Entry will show.

Cancel Booking

By Agency Owner

  1. Go to the “Booking” menu.

  2. Select the “category: car or hotel”.

  3. Entry will show.

  4. Click on the “action” option.

  5. Detail will see.

  6. Click on the “cancel booking”

Or

  1. Click on the “View Detail” option.

  2. Select the reason why cancel the booking.

  3. Click on the “cancel booking”

  4. Booking will cancel.
