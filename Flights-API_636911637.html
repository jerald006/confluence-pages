Purpose

Offer a list of endpoints to support the Flight transactions:

  • Shopping (Offer-management)

  • Bookings (Order-management)

It is required to create the structure of the endpoints according to the industry standards accomplish the AIDM bases of IATA.

Concepts introduction

Offer-Management

It refers the to the search offer criteria and the management of the offer to expose to consumers
The main service associated to the offer-management is the Shopping

Order-management

Concept

An order refers to a single or group of services booked for a traveler under s single identification instance. Internally and order is a container instance for a single or group of traveler that hold different services inside, it is a concept related to Cart of services:

Flight components:

The legacy concept of Travel industry is the PNR (Passenger Name record)
In the case of an Order management the PNR is part of the entire Order, the flights component have their own identification Booking Reference or Booking Code, to identify the portion of the Order.

In todayÂ´s operation of travel industry, the components of an order could become from different vendors (Actors), inside the Flight components, the entire itinerary can hold different flight segments from different vendors as NDC explained in their model.
For XENI purposes, this concept will be the end of the roadmap, but in the first approach to the distribution and booking order concept, the flight component booked will hold the services from an specific vendor.
