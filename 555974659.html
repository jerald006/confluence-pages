XeniApp : TEMPLATE: Questions for new agency sign-ups

Created by User d0da9 on Feb 13, 2023

Intro:
We want to understand our customers better, learn about their pains, their needs and their expectations from XENI. We want to use this information to increase XENI’s competitive advantage and the value for our customers. Also, we want to develop personae, use our customer’s language on our website and tailor our products, services and marketing initiatives more specifically.


How To:

  • Before talking to a new customer, copy this template page to a new page and rename your copy using the name of your customer.

  • When talking to a new sign-up, go through these questions. Answering our customer’s questions always should be first!

  1. What are your goals from using Xeni?  (e.g. scale, access to inventory, quick tech solution, clients want web portal, etc?)


  2. Are you hosted or unhosted or member of a travel consortia? 

    1. If unhosted, why haven’t you decided to join a host agency?

       

    2. If hosted: what is your host agency? What additional benefits do you hope to get from Xeni?


  3. If talking to a Host Agency: How many agents belong to your Host Agency?


  4. How many travelers do you service a month/a year?


     

  5. How much bookings volume do you typically do a year (or if new, hope to do a year)

    1. flights

    2. hotels

    3. packages

    4. other (excursions, etc.)

  6. What markets do you primarily cater to (destination or origin markets)?


  7. What other technology solutions have you looked at other than Xeni?  Why did you decide to go with Xeni?


  8. Which technologies have you been using before?

    1. What did you like?


    2. What did you not like?



9. Would you like to participate in focus groups where we concentrate on future development of our platform together with our platform?
