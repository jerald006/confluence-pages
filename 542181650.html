XeniApp : [BTC]How to Create a wallet on bitpay for Testing with Bitcoin

Created by User 9c00b, last modified on Nov 08, 2022

For downloading the Bitpay wallet

Go to Bitpay.com and chose download options as per Your OS Only the following coins have testnet capabilities:

Bitcoin (BTC)

Bitcoin Cash (BCH)

Ethereum (ETH) (Does not include ERC20 stablecoins.) Ripple (XRP)

The following steps are valid for all the coins as mentioned earlier.

1. Click the ··· icon in the Wallet tab of the app.

2. Click Create a new wallet.

3. Select Simple Wallet.

4. Select the currency. Remember that USD tokens are not supported for testnet.

5. Name the wallet (1), and tap Show advanced options (2).

6. Enable Testnet (1) and tap CREATE (2).

7. You can choose to protect the wallet with a password if you like.

8. The testnet wallet has now been created.

A gray icon currency indicates testnet wallets on the wallets tab (1) and a small flask symbol and a TESTNET text on the wallet view (2). If you see the flask symbol, you are dealing with a testnet wallet

Now that you have a wallet set up, you are ready to receive some testnet coins. Testnet bitcoins carry no value, and cannot be used to pay monetary transactions. They are solely for testing transactions and implementations.

1. First, you will need to get the receiving address for your testnet wallet. Click Receive at the bottom of the screen (1) while in the testnet wallet, then click the address at the bottom to copy it to your phone or computer’s clipboard (2).

2. Navigate to https://testnet.coinfaucet.eu/en/ or your choice of testnet faucet, paste your testnet address, click Get Bitcoins! and solve the captcha.

3. Within a few minutes, you should see the pending transaction, and a balance in your testnet wallet.

Note - For Test BTC, you can claim from the below links

https://coinfaucet.eu/en/btc-testnet/

https://bitcoinfaucet.uo1.net/send.php

https://testnet-faucet.com/btc-testnet/
