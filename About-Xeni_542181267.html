Introduction

 This document aims to serve as the consolidated business requirements for a platform called Xeni, which gives end-to-end solutions to online travel sellers to sell their travel offers to customers under own brand at ease of convenience.

Xeni is an online platform for travel agents where they can create customized websites and earn revenue via Flights, Hotels, Cars, and Activities bookings. Xeni is the solution for small travel companies under a single umbrella. Here they can manage their revenue, clients (Consumers), Sales agents, etc.

Business & project objective

 Xeni addresses the major pain points in the travel industry supply chain specifically involving the sale of online travel inventory. Through its B2B platform, Xeni facilitates online selling of travel, access to wholesale-rate inventory, and reduction of payment fraud, cost and settlement time. 

Needs statement

 Xeni is an operating system for travel powering anyone seeking to sell travel online. Our system allows our clients to instantly launch a web-based storefront, a booking engine with wholesale travel inventory, a custom-for-travel CRM, and a blockchain based payment settlement engine.

The purpose of this application is to provide small travel companies a platform where they can create their own website and generate revenue through Flights bookings, Hotel bookings, Car bookings, and Activity bookings for their clients (Consumers). These travel companies also get a dashboard where they can manage their earnings (Commissions), Bookings, clients, and sales representatives (Sales Agents).

Personas

There are three broad categories of user personas involved in the Xeni application. 

  • Travel Agency / Company 

  • Sales Agents

  • End User (clients)

Agency Signup

  • Agent Information

    1. Travel sellers can be on-boarded using simple signup process, where they need to register their email address and travel company/agency details. To complete the agency onboarding, travel sellers must subscribe to a subscription.

    2. Payment Information

      • To complete the profile setup, the travel seller must make the subscription payment first. Once the payment is completed, they can see the default view of the dashboard. The platform will accept only Fiat currency.

    3. Coupon Code Information

    4. Terms and Conditions

      • As a part of sign up process end user must agree to the Terms and conditions of Xeni platform.

User Signup

  • Xeni as a platform facilitates Flight booking, hotel booking and Car rental to end users.  End user can register on Xeni with name, email to avail the offerings on platform. As a part of sign up process end user must agree to the Terms and conditions of Xeni platform.

Flight Booking

  • For the Flight ticket booking based on the filters, the data need to be filled in, in order to book the flight ticket.  Users can chose their filter as per need, for example user can choose one way trip or Round trip, similarly users can select number of travelers and choose type of cabin like Economy, Business, Premium, First. Once the user clicks on the search button all the available flights will be shown as per the filters.

    1. The users have to add multiple flight boarding and destination details. User can add a maximum of 3 destinations. (This option is only available in case of the multi-city)

Hotel Booking

  • Hotel is another booking option available under Book Now. Once the owner clicks on the hotels they can view the booking form.

    1. The user enters the location

    2. Once click on the Dates calendar will be displayed. The users should be able to select multiple dates.

    3. Room and Number of Travelers

      • On the click of room and traveler, a popup will appear in which the owner will select the Number of rooms and number of adults.

      • In a single room 4 adults and 4 children can be selected maximum. As the number of children gets selected the age selection box will appear. Where they have to select the age of each child.

      • Once the owner clicks on the added room 2 should appear where they will enter the traveler details for the room. 

    4. Once all the data is selected the owner will search for the hotels available for that dates in the searched location. All the available hotels will be displayed and from there they can book.

Car Rental

  • Under the cars, the owners will be able to book the cars based on the filters. 

    1. The owner will select the country, Select the drop-off location, and the driver's age.

    2. Pick up location

    3. Dates From to To location

    4. Select date

    5. Search 

    6. Once the owner clicks on the search for all the available cars from the city, dates will be displayed.

Command Center

Dashboard

Gives overview of agency analytics, IT consists of Total Confirmed Bookings, Upcoming Trips, Total Bookings, Cancelled Bookings, Total Commission.

Book Now

Agency can book flights, hotels and rent cars. Flight Booking, Hotel Booking an Renting cars is briefed above.

Clients

Clients are the end users. The travel agents will Book the Tickets, Accommodation, Cars, and Activities for the client. For making bookings on their own, the clients will have to first sign up on the agency website. The clients can sign up for the 'Sign Up' feature on the travel agent's website

The travel agency can turn clients into their sales agents on the Xeni platform and they can decide the commission percentage of each booking to the sales agent.

Agents

Agency can have sales agent who can sell the bookings representing agency. Once the travel agency is on boarded on the Xeni they can invite their sales agents to the platform. Sales agents can only be invited by the Travel Agency owner (Account Owner). The travel agency can see each sales agent's how many bookings they have made. The agency can decide the commission percentage for the bookings those sales agents have made or leave nil.

Bookings

Under the bookings, the agency owner will be able to all the bookings made by the agency. Including Bookings by Clients, Sales Agents, and himself. 

In the booking history, the agency can view the booking with below fields,

  • Record Locator 

  • From 

  • To 

  • Date Booked 

  • Travel Date 

  • Cost Price 

  • Sales Price 

  • Commission Earned 

  • Transaction Fees

  • Penalty Amount 

  • Booking Status 

    • Canceled

    • Booked

    • Ticketed 

    • Ticket Requested

Action 

  • upon clicking action Icon, the owner will be able to see the flight details. Please check the below Image for reference.

The Agency Owner can search for the particular flight's bookings by the record locator. 

The agency owner will be able to filter the bookings by

  • Date (Calendar pop-up)

    • Customized 

  • Category 

    • Flight 

    • Hotels 

    • Cars

  • Filters 

    • Show past trips (toggle)

    • All

    • Ticketed

    • Ticket Requested

    • Booked

    • Canceled

Note: Status filter options would change as per the category- different for Flights, Cars, and Hotels

Export 

  • When the agency owner clicks on the export a pop-up calendar will be opened. The data can be exported for the previous months, not for future dates. The user can select the date range.

Settings

  • Agency Settings

    • Agency can update markup settings of Hotel booking and car rentals.

    • On Xeni the travel seller can define their commission based on the Bookings e.g. for Flight Bookings they can set a 10% commission on each booking, the same goes for Hotel Bookings, Self-Drive Cars. It's all up to the agency what percentage of commission they wish to charge from the users. 

      • The Travel Agency not only can define their commission based on Bookings but also can define per client. Following are examples.

        • User one is booking a ticket for a flight from Mumbai to Delhi. Here the Travel Agency is charging 10% commission.

        • User Two is booking a ticket for the same flight. Here they are charging a 5% commission.

      • The travel sellers have the freedom to set the commission for each Booking type or for each client.

  • Website Settings

    • Under the website settings, the Agency Owner can change or customize their website. They can decide the website theme, color they wish, banners, terms, and conditions, privacy policy, cookie policies, disclaimer, customizing agency name, contact supports and DNS, etc. Agency owners also have the option to enter their social media links which will be shown on the user's side.

  • Profile Settings

    • Agency can update personal information of agency and change password using profile settings.

  • Payment Settings

    • Payment information can be updated from payment setting.
