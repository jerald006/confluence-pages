XeniApp : End Point - API Details ( For Reference)

Created by User d8ec2 on Jun 09, 2021

POST /v1/auth/login/{AgentCode here}  -- for Login

POST /v1/quote    --- for getting Quick quote 

POST /v1/quote   --- for getting quote with coverages

POST /v1/quote  --- for getting quote with policy buyer

POST /v1/quote   --- for getting quote with flights

POST /v1/eligibility  --- to check for policy eligibility

POST /v1/purchase  ---   to purchase the policy

GET /v1/policy/{policyNumber}   ---  to get policy info

GET /v2/policy/edit/{policynumber}  --  to edit the policy

POST /v1/policy/editcheck   --- to check whether the policy can be edited

POST /v1/policy/editconfirm  --- to confirm after editing the policy and change the info

POST /v1/policy/cancelcheck   --- to check whether the policy can be cancelled

POST /v1/policy/cancelconfirm  -- to confirm the policy cancellation

GET /v1/Licenses/{agentCode}  --- to check the license info of a agent.
