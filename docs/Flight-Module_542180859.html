XeniApp : Flight Module

Created by vaibhav (Unlicensed), last modified on Aug 12, 2021

Product Goals

  1. Provide the best B2B Rates to the travel Agents

  2. Provide a wide range of inventory options, include LCC as well

  3. Ability to book ancillary services like seat, extra luggage, sports equipment, etc

  4. Ability to reprice and reshop on the platform

  5. Ability to book multiple flights in one checkout

  6. Poll Booked flight to check for any changes

Business Goals

  1. Provide Private fares to the Travel Agents

  2. Automatically rebook a Flight when the price drops
