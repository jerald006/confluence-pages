Functional objective

All the reservations created through Flight Product must include the SSR:FOID to avoid any issues at the time of ticketing.

Context

The airlines and the Airlines alliances are offering a lot of incentives to the traveler based on their frequent traveler programs:

The fidelity of the passengers allow them to access discounts and benefits based on the Tier level (Fidelity level) that have impact in several different offers like seatmaps, upgrades, ancillaries, etc.

MVP Scope

  • Collect the Frequent traveler programs passenger affiliated. (1-3)

  • Identify the applicable segments. (Airline/s in the program)

  • Associate the FQTV to the right segments/passenger.
