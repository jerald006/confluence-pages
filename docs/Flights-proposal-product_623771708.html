Purpose

The main purpose of this proposal is to rebuild the Flight Product of the Xeni APP, and remove Thomalex dependency as a provider of exclusive content.

The scope of the solution should be faced in several different levels based on an extensive strategy to be align with the industry standards and customer needs. It includes changes in the way to get the content,, creating a new integration, getting the right data to analyze the behavior of our consumers and do it scalable.

This product will give XENI more flexibility and the power of decide over the content to display by based on logic data of consumption and preferences.
