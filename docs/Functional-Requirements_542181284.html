Xeni OTA

Store Booking details on Blockchain

As a Xeni OTA, I want my customer bookings to be stored on blockchain so that I can verify my booking transactions.

Pre-requisite

  • Customer has made a travel booking

Redeem Earnings

As a Xeni OTA, I want to redeem my earnings from Xeni so that I get the money that I earned.

Pre-requisite

  • Customer has made a travel booking

Transfer Fees Instantly

As a Xeni OTA, I want the correct booking fees to be transferred to the correct stakeholders instantly so that everyone gets what their share.

Design my travel website

As a Xeni OTA, I want to be able to design my own travel website so that my customers can make their travel bookings.
