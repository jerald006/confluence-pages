XeniApp : Lock destinations

Created by Gerardo Nizetich on Apr 30, 2024
  • Objective
  • Requirement
  • Services scope

Objective

Give the Agency owner the capability to setup a restricted Market in the APP

It will include restriction levels by:

  • City

  • State/province

  • Regions (Group of cities and states)

  • Country

Requirement

We need the analysis to develop the lock destination feature on the Command center.
The goal is to allow Agency Owners to limit the destinations offering in the UI by filtering them by Region, Country, State or City, plus the functionality to create custom Zones. Not only we have several business opportunities on hold waiting for this enhancements, but also will help us offer a solution for Destination Management Organizations.
