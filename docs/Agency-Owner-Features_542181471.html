Create an Agency

An agency owner can create their own agency by paying the subscription fees. After creating an agency, the agency owner gets access to their dashboard known as the “Command Center”.

Edit Settings

Agency Settings

Agency owners are given the option to add a “Markup Fees” to various services including:

  • Accommodations

  • Car Rentals

Currently, markup fees for flights is not supported due to some complications.

Website Settings

The agency owner can decide the look and feel of their website. To customise that, they can edit the following options:

  • Agency Name

  • Upload Logo

  • Decide Color Theme

  • Upload Banner Image

  • Enter Website Heading

  • Enter Website Sub-Heading

  • Set Social Media Links

  • Privacy Policy

  • Cookies Policy

  • Disclaimer

  • Contact Support

Whitelist Signup domain

The agency owner can decide to only allow signup from particular domains. They can add a list of domains to the whitelist option. Only emails belonging to the whitelisted domains can then create an account on the agency owner’s website.

Custom Domain

The agency owner can choose to use their custom domain for their website. To do that, they can enter their full domain name. The request would then be send to the support team who will port the website for the agency owner.

Currently, this is a manual process for the support team. This will be automated in the future.

Profile Settings

Set your profile info with the information such as:

  • Name

  • Email

  • Phone Number

  • Location

  • Password

Payment Settings

If the agency owner wishes to change the card details that they had provided during signup, they can do that.
