XeniApp : Confirm Option

Created by Gerardo Nizetich, last modified on Jun 17, 2024

Overview

The endpoint allows the consumer to reconfirm the availability of the option and in case of success return the list of extra services offered in the itinerary.

Requisites

  • FareSourceCode: The unique price identification Key of the itinerary for the vendor

  • ConversationId: Unique identification key of the user session.

  • Target: The proper environment of the content.
