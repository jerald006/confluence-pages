Overview

Generate a dynamic Cache of shopping offers by Market/Vendor.

The cache limit could be 30 mins depending on the services offered by vendors.
The general idea of the proposal is to reduce the number of request to the shopping service methods of the vendors and concentrate the cache over the top 10 origin destinations consumed by customers.

It is a required condition to have the right metrics and measure the behavior of the consumers by market to identify those top 10 O&D, Highly dates of consumption, preferred carrier and the vendor.

It is a nice to have for each vendor to offer a method to confirm the availability of a fareID (Segments + Fare + Type of Passenger + carrier + conditions of fare + available space).
