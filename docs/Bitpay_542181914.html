XeniApp : Bitpay

Created by Praveen Shivalingaiah, last modified by Vivek Agrawal on Nov 16, 2022

The BitPay service is created to communicate with BitPay services, store transactional information.

BitPay’s integrations make accepting blockchain payments fast and reliable.

BitPay converts the cryptocurrencies to your preferred currency and adds the full amount of the payment to your next BitPay account settlement. Funds are then deposited to your bank account or cryptocurrency wallet.

What is Webhook?

Webhook are automated messages sent from apps when something happens.

How does webhook in Bitpay work?

Webhooks are HTTP POST messages sent from the BitPay server to the merchant’s eCommerce server.

In Bitpay the only webhook which is available is Instant Payment Notification.

The purpose of an IPN is to alert merchant's ecommerce server that the
status of the resource (invoice,payout) has changed.

content-Type: application/json

Payment states

  1. paid

  2. confirmed

  3. complete

Step 3: Endpoint is configured to read event objects for the type of
