XeniApp : Phocus Wright

Created by Praveen Shivalingaiah on Nov 02, 2022

·         Participant onboarding:

o   Specific QR code which redirects specific OTA endpoint ( eg: hederatravel.com) ( specific QR for specific OTA)

o   Register with name, email  to participate ( email is mandatory field)

o   Registered email will receive OTP

o   Validate with OTP received on registered email

·         Wallet :

o   generateWallet for participant

 

·         Securing wallet: store private keys safely

·         Avatars: participant has to choose pre-defined avatars

·         Purchase tickets: Participant has purchase contest related flight tickets or holiday packages.

 

·         3 random winners: have to pick three random winners

 

 

 

 

Tech tasks:

  •  

generateQR() : QR code for the hederatravel.com

  •  

emailMagicLink() :  Send contest details to registered email upon registering.

  •  

generateWallet(): creates publickey and privatekeys for specific user. Store public and private keys in local DB synchronously.

  •  

createAvatar(): custom set of avatars ( need to discuss more on type of avatars)

  •  

participate:  every participant gets xbucks – 500 , Hbar – 10( also represent in USD value), xenicoins – 10 upon requesting ( display request button)

o   xbucks mint- upon enrolling

o   xbucks burn – upon purchasing

  •  

listOfOTAPackages: packages offered by OTA to participants for the contest.

  •  

getParticipants:  list of all participants who successfully purchased packages from contest.

  •  

selectWinner() : Picking random three winners from participants who successfully purchased packages from contest

 

 

 

 

 

 

Suggestion:

 

Instead of having separate QR for each OTA, can we have a common QR which has list of all OTA offering packages for the contest (dropdown). Upon selecting a specific OTA, participant will be redirected to its website.
