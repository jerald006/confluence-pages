XeniApp : Xeni Points - Credit Feature

Created by vaibhav (Unlicensed) on Jan 04, 2023

Introduction

The credit feature is designed to provide travel agency owners with an alternative payment option for booking travel services. Instead of paying for bookings with credit cards or crypto, agency owners can use Xeni points to cover the cost of bookings.

The credit feature allows agency owners and sales agents to use points to cover the cost of bookings, either in full or in conjunction with other forms of payment such as cash or credit.

Currently, Each Xeni Point will be equal to one dollar.

Target Audience

  1. Agency owner

    1. It allows agency owners to manage their Xeni points and make bookings with them. This feature will give agency owners the ability to view their available points balance, redeem points for bookings, and track their point usage.

      Here are some specific capabilities that agency owners may have with the credit feature:

      • View their available points balance: Agency owners will be able to see how many points they have available to use for bookings. This will allow them to plan their point usage and make informed decisions about when to redeem points.

      • Redeem points for bookings: Agency owners will be able to use their points to cover the cost of bookings directly from their agency's account. This will be a simple and convenient way for them to make bookings.

      • Track point usage: Agency owners will be able to view their point usage history, including the date and time of each transaction, the number of points redeemed, and the value of the booking in cash or credit. This will allow them to track their point usage and also distribute the points amongst the various sales agent in the agency.

  2. Sales Agent

    1. Sales agents within a travel agency to make bookings via the Xeni points. This feature will give sales agents the ability to use points to cover the cost of bookings on behalf of their agency's customers.

      Here are some specific capabilities that sales agents may have with the credit feature:

      • View available points balance: Sales agents will be able to see how many points are available to use for bookings within their agency. This will allow them to plan their point usage and make informed decisions about when to redeem points.

      • Redeem points for bookings: Sales agents will be able to use their agency's points to cover the cost of bookings directly from the agency's account. This will be a simple and convenient way for them to make bookings on behalf of their customers.

      • Track point usage: Sales agents will be able to view the point usage history for their agency, including the date and time of each transaction, the amount of points redeemed, and the value of the booking in cash or credit. This will allow them to track their point usage and budget accordingly.

User Flow

  1. The agency owner or sales agent logs in to their agency's account.

  2. They choose the type of booking they want to make (e.g., flight, hotel, etc.).

  3. They enter the details of the booking (e.g., destination, dates, number of travelers, etc.).

  4. The agency owner or sales agent selects "Pay with points" as the payment method.

  5. The system displays the available points balance for the agency.

  6. The agency owner or sales agent selects the number of points they want to redeem for the booking.

  7. If the points are not sufficient to cover the full cost of the booking, the agency owner or sales agent can choose to combine points with credit or crypto to pay for the remaining amount.

  8. The agency owner or sales agent confirms the booking and the points are deducted from their available balance.

  9. The booking is confirmed and the agency owner or sales agent receives a confirmation email.

Feature Extension

The super admin to set up rules for awarding points to agencies based on bookings made via credit or crypto:

With this extension, the super admin will be able to create rules that specify the number of points that an agency should receive for each booking made using credit or crypto. These rules could be based on factors such as the type of booking, the value of the booking, or the agency's overall performance.

For example, the super admin might set up a rule that awards 50 points to an agency for every flight booking made using credit, and another rule that awards 100 points to an agency for every hotel booking made using crypto. The super admin could also set up rules that award bonus points to high-performing agencies or that incentivize agencies to use credit or crypto more frequently. Every point can translate to a dollar value which can be decided.

To implement this feature extension, the system will need to track the payment methods used for each booking and automatically apply the appropriate points rules. The system will also need to update the points balances of the relevant agencies in real-time as bookings are made.

This feature extension will provide the super admin with more control over the points program and will allow them to use points as a way to incentivize and reward agency performance. It will also provide agencies with an additional way to earn points and will encourage them to use credit and crypto as payment methods for bookings.
