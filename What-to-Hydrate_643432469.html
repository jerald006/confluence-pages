Passenger Types
Based on the vendor’s explanation, every cruise line should have their own passenger type policy and in some occasions they have different passenger types based on a Sailing. 
We need to get this information at the time of sign an agreement with a cruise line and create a cruise line Profile to get the details of what is consider as 
ADULTS
CHILDREN
INFANT
per cruise line and make it dynamically. 
The constraint here is what happen when the user makes a default search without selecting a cruise line. 
Cruise Details page
The small pictures beside the Cruise Image, should be representative images of the interior of the ship. 
Itinerary Tab
The itinerary tab of each ship is returned from /cruisecabingrades.pl, the sailing of a ship is not normally changed, so we can store in cache the itinerary associated to a ship after the first response.
