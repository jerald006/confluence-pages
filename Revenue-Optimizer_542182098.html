  1. Introduction

    1. Objective

Revenue Optimizer is an optional service provided to agency owners of the platform to increase their potential revenue. Along with existing recommendations system will show revenue optimization suggestions to agency owners

  1. Intended Audience

This feature is available for all agency owners who are offering packages using the platform.

  1. Project Scope

2.1 In Scope Functionality

Revenue optimizations are suggested  for each offering made by agency owners on a platform. System takes care of optimization by calling priceline service, gets the latest prices on priceline and recommends better fare details to agency owners. Agency owners have the option to sort with a  ‘max revenue’ filter.

    4. Business Process Overview

4.1 Proposed Business Process(To-Be)

  • Agency owners can choose Revenue Optimizer from their dashboard at their own convenience. 

  • Upon opting Revenue Optimizer, agency owners can see revenue optimization features while they are offering packages on the website. 

  • System will make necessary calculations for the offering listed by the agency owner and adjust the markup fees of the agency owner to increase potential revenue for that particular offering.  

  • The agency owner can see the Xeni’s rate, agency owner markup fee and customer rate for each offering listed  by them on the platform.

 

  1. Business Requirements

5.1 Functional Requirements

  • Option to choose Revenue Optimizer in agency owner dashboard

  • Offered prices from priceline

  • Max revenue sorting filter

  • Displaying Xeni’s rate, agency owner markup fee, customer rate.

  • Potential revenue increase (markup for max revenue)
