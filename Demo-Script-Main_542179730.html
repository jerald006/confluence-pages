XeniApp : Demo Script Main

Created by vaibhav (Unlicensed) on Dec 29, 2021

  1. Go to the pricing page - https://xeni.booking.xeniapp.net/pricing

  2. Enter all the details of the company

  3. You will be redirected to the Payment Page. Download the Terms and Condition Document

  4. Put the payment information on the next page. Agency will get created.

  5. Login to the Agency. Move to the settings tab and click on edit website.

  6. Upload logo, Banner, change color

  7. Invite Agent. You will have to verify the email address

  8. Go to the Booking Engine - Search Flight, Hotels, Car rental, and add to cart.

  9. Click on check out and show the multi-checkout form.

  10. Sign up as a client and log in. Show the dashboard feature
