XeniApp Website

For users on XeniApp, they can one of 4 possible roles:

A user can only have one role assigned to them.

Admin

Admin is the owner of XeniApp. They use the Admin Dashboard to view the entire application at a glance and manage Agency Owners.

Admin is also known as Super Admin.

Agency Owner

The person who creates and owns an Agency on Xeni. They choose sub-domain to manage. They pay a subscription fees to provide travel services to their customers.

Sales Agent

They work for Agency Owner. They make sales on behalf of the agency. They have their own set of permissions. They are a company representative.

Client

Users who login to an agency are called clients. They are the customers for an agency who purchase services from the platform.

External

Vendors

Vendors are the parties that provide information about various service providers. These vendors expose a set of APIs which are integrated in XeniApp to fetch the required information.

Vendors are also referred to as Consolidators.
