Pro & cons

Pro

  • Good GDS content

  • Including KIU content for Latin America

  • Detailed Net Fares and Public Fares

  • Good detail of Fares

  • Hold bookings (Ticket time limit)

  • Ticketing API

  • Non-void API

  • Split payment.

  • Multi-payment.

Cons

  • Not support Seat maps

  • Support only Ancillaries Baggage

  • No Cancel service (It is required to Queue the Reservation to cancel)

  • Non Update booking itinerary

  • Non Fare Construction details in Shopping Service

  • The booking flow must be completed at time of the booking creation.

  • Queue for void ticket.
