XeniApp : Shopping Cart implementation

Created by Gerardo Nizetich on Nov 20, 2023

Objective

Numerate the conditions of services to be added to the Shopping cart

Type of services

The order of the services doesnÂ´t matter, the user can start from any service.

The platform should interpret where the user starts to add services and offer the option to book the next one like:

Start point: Air

Next: Would you like to add a Hotel?

Next: Would you like to rent-a-car?

Next: Look for activities in the data range and offer: Would you like to add an Activity? (Show the list of activities in the destination)

Flight services

  • Fare conditions for Ticketing (Instant Purchase vs Hold Reservation)

  • User selects the flight itinerary

    • Add the passenger/s information

    • Create the book and map the Ticket Time limit of the reservation.

    • Display a Warning Popup advising consumer: Your flights book expires on (Ticket Time limit - (30 Minutes).

  • Constraints:

    • Does our suppliers accept the booking without payment detail?

    • Does our supplier add the 10 minutes TKTL for fulfill the reservation information before cancelling (Instant Purchase case)

    • Does our supplier send Instant purchase flag to identify those cases?
