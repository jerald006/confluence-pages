Structure

  • Information about the policy comes back under the policy node of the response.

  • travelers are returned in their own top-level node.

  • coverages are returned beneath each traveler. This is because each traveler is covered and each coverage applies to each traveler

  • flights are returned in their own top-level node.

Policy Information

  • status: The status of a policy

  • canBeEdited: indicates whether or not the policy can be edited at the time of the request

  • canBeCancelled: indicates if the policy is eligible to be cancelled at the time of the request

  • canBeRefunded: indicates if the customer is eligible for a refund if the policy is cancelled. There are cases in which the policy may be cancelled but the customer may not be eligible for a refund

Statuses

Status

Meaning

Active

The policy has been purchased but no coverages are yet in effect

Effective

At least one of the coverages are in effect

Expired

The All coverages are no longer effective

Cancelled

The consumer cancelled the policy

Flight Information

This is the same information as the customer entered but it also contains the local time of the departure at that airport.
